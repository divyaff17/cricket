About the Academy

Not a fancy talk academy. More like "come to nets and grind".

Elite Cricket Academy is for players who want real improvement — technique, mindset, and consistency. We train school-level, club-level and anyone who is serious.

Sessions include batting, bowling, fielding drills, fitness routines and match practice. We also push discipline (on time, warm-up, stretching… boring but needed).
